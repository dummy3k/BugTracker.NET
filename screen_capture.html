Download the latest version of the BugTracker.NET Screen Capture Utilityfrom Sourceforge.

The BugTracker.NET Screen Capture Utility lets you capture a screen shot, annotate it, and post it to BugTracker.NET in just a few clicks.

Before you can use the utility, you need to click on "Configure..." to tell it where your BugTracker.NET website is and who you are. If you want, you can get a head start by generating a ".reg" file here with your initial settings. Click to generate it, then save it and click on it, to import your initial settings to your registry.
